# LovePrint Archetypes

## 1. The Growth Partner
**Hook:** If they’re not evolving, you’re losing interest.  
**Focus:** Growth, ambition, alignment.  
**Strength:** Strong long-term potential.  
**Risk:** Can turn relationships into performance reviews.

## 2. The Chemistry Chaser
**Hook:** If it doesn’t feel intense, it doesn’t feel real.  
**Focus:** Passion, spark, emotional intensity.  
**Strength:** Excitement and energy.  
**Risk:** Volatility and instability.

## 3. The Balanced Seeker
**Hook:** You’re not asking for everything… but it’s close.  
**Focus:** Attraction, connection, and stability.  
**Strength:** Well-rounded standards.  
**Risk:** May eliminate compatible partners too quickly.

## 4. The Builder
**Hook:** You don’t date for fun—you date for a future.  
**Focus:** Stability, structure, long-term partnership.  
**Strength:** Strong foundation.  
**Risk:** May lack spontaneity.

## 5. The Social Strategist
**Hook:** You don’t just want love—you want a partner who fits your life.  
**Focus:** Presentation, communication, lifestyle alignment.  
**Strength:** Social compatibility.  
**Risk:** Can become image-focused.

## 6. The Power Player
**Hook:** You don’t want average—you want intensity.  
**Focus:** Status, leadership, chemistry.  
**Strength:** High drive and passion.  
**Risk:** Relationships may burn hot and burn out fast.

## 7. The Charisma Seeker
**Hook:** You want connection, chemistry, and security all at once.  
**Focus:** Communication, intimacy, stability.  
**Strength:** Strong emotional and physical potential.  
**Risk:** Expectations may be unrealistic.

## 8. The Lifestyle Curator
**Hook:** You want a relationship that feels good every day.  
**Focus:** Comfort, attraction, ease.  
**Strength:** Low-friction compatibility.  
**Risk:** May lack depth under pressure.

## 9. The Sensory Addict
**Hook:** You follow the vibe—and the vibe runs your relationships.  
**Focus:** Physical attraction and experience.  
**Strength:** Strong chemistry.  
**Risk:** Low long-term stability.

## 10. The Idealist
**Hook:** Your standards are elite… your results may not be.  
**Focus:** High standards across multiple traits.  
**Strength:** Clear expectations.  
**Risk:** Chronic dissatisfaction.
