# Result Outputs

## Serious Mode Structure
1. Archetype name
2. One-line hook
3. Scores
4. What this means
5. Strength
6. Risk
7. Relationship prediction
8. What they actually need
9. Share CTA

## Funny Mode Structure
1. Archetype name
2. Playful hook
3. Funny score labels
4. What this means
5. Strength
6. Problem
7. Relationship prediction
8. What they actually need

## Example: The Growth Partner

### Serious
**You are: The Growth Partner**  
“If they’re not evolving, you’re losing interest.”

You are driven by progress. You want a partner who is improving physically, mentally, emotionally, and financially. You’re not just looking for love—you’re looking for alignment.

**Strength:** You create relationships that can actually grow.  
**Risk:** You may turn relationships into performance evaluations.  
**Prediction:** Stable and forward-moving, but may lack spontaneity.  
**Need:** Someone who values growth but also brings emotional warmth and fun.

### Funny
**You are: The Growth Partner**  
“You don’t fall in love… you run performance reviews.”

You want someone with goals, discipline, hydration, a workout plan, and probably a five-year vision board.

**Strength:** You build power couples.  
**Problem:** Relax. Not everything is a self-improvement project.  
**Prediction:** Great relationship until you start giving quarterly reviews.  
**Need:** Someone who grows with you but reminds you to enjoy life.
