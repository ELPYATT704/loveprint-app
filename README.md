## 💡 What is LovePrint? + Purpose Statement

LovePrint is designed to address a growing gap in modern relationships: people are dating more, but understanding themselves—and what they truly need in a partner—less. 
In the U.S., marriage rates have declined significantly over the past several decades, with fewer adults choosing to marry and more experiencing long-term singleness or delayed partnerships (Pew Research Center). 
At the same time, loneliness has been identified as a public health concern, with studies from U.S. Surgeon General highlighting increasing rates of social isolation, particularly among adults navigating life transitions
such as divorce, career shifts, or aging without a partner. Additionally, remarriage rates remain relatively low—many individuals who lose or leave a long-term partner never remarry,
often due to uncertainty about what to look for next or how to avoid repeating past patterns.

LovePrint addresses this problem by helping users uncover their relationship archetype, attraction biases, and behavioral tendencies through an interactive, AI-powered experience. 
Instead of offering generic advice, it creates personalized insights that bridge the gap between what people say they want and how they actually choose partners. 
The goal is not just entertainment, but clarity—helping users make more intentional relationship decisions, improve compatibility, and ultimately reduce the cycle of misaligned partnerships. 
By combining behavioral psychology, self-reflection, and shareable insights, LovePrint transforms dating from a guessing game into a more informed and self-aware process.

LovePrint helps people understand:
- Their relationship archetype
- What they prioritize in partners
- Why their relationships succeed or fail
- How compatible they are with others
